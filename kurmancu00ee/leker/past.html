Dema Borî (Past Tenses)

Kurdish has two main past tenses: Simple Past (Dema Borî ya Hêsan) for completed actions in the past, and Past Perfect (Dema Borî ya Pêşketî) for actions that had happened before another past action.

Simple Past (Dema Borî ya Hêsan)

Usage:

Used for completed actions in the past.

Formation:

Verb stem + past tense personal endings

Verb Conjugations

1. bûn (to be)
Pronoun Conjugation English
Ez bûm I was
Tu bûyî You were
Ew bû He/She was
Em bûn We were
Hûn bûn You were
Ew bûn They were
2. heye (to have)
Pronoun Conjugation English
Min ... hebû hebû I had
Te ... hebû hebû You had
Wî/Wê ... hebû hebû He/She had
Me ... hebû hebû We had
We ... hebû hebû You had
Wan ... hebû hebû They had
